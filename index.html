Who We Are

Sebadom Enterprise

We are a milk processing firm based in Mbeya, Tanzania. Established in 2014, We produce healthy dairy products that seeks to serve the gap in healthy milk produce across the region.

We have an offering of fresh milk, yoghurt, mtindi, that is readily available and a variety of cheese provided at a per order basis.

Why We operate

Driven By Healthy Milk

From inception back in 2014, We noticed a gap in healthy milk across the region. Milk produced at a higher healthy standard was lacking. We set out to consult with Heifer International who are worldwide experts in livestock, farming and agriculture in relation to its economics as a whole. We set out to lead in producing quality milk across the region.

We have gone to produce 5 litres of milk upto 500 litres From years 2014 through 2020.
